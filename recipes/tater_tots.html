Tater Tots

Description

Skip the freezer aisle and make this tater tot recipe at home. They’re so much more delicious than the store-bought stuff!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: Start with two medium russet potatoes.
  • Parsley: Fresh parsley adds color and flavor.
  • Flour: All-purpose flour helps hold the tots together and is essential for crispiness.
  • Seasonings: The tater tots are seasoned with salt, seasoned pepper, and onion powder.
  • Oil: Peanut oil is perfect for frying homemade tater tots.

Steps

  1. Boil, then drain, the potatoes.
  2. Shred the potatoes and drain the excess moisture.
  3. Mix the potatoes with parsley, flour, and seasonings.
  4. Shape the mixture into tots.
  5. Fry the tots until they’re golden and crispy.
